Temple Development

The sacred Perne Kshetra is well maintained and hosts a large number of devotees. Temple runs completely through the liberal and pious donations from the devotees. There is a lot of scope for improvement of Kshetra and provide better facilities to the people. We appeal to you to make the generous contribution towards the development of the temple and give us strength.
